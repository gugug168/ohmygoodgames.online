How to Play

Getting Started

Use the arrow keys or WASD to move your character around the map. Your goal is to survive as long as possible against waves of monsters.

Combat & Abilities

Your character attacks automatically when monsters are in range. Collect experience points to level up and choose new abilities or upgrades.

Strategy Tips

Keep moving to avoid being surrounded. Prioritize upgrading your favorite weapons and defensive abilities. Don't forget to collect health items when your HP is low!

About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an exciting arcade-style survival game where players must fend off endless waves of monsters. Inspired by popular roguelike games, Monster Survivors offers a challenging yet addictive gameplay experience that will keep you coming back for more.

In Monster Survivors, you'll start with basic abilities and gradually become more powerful as you collect experience points and upgrades. Choose your path wisely as you develop your character's unique build to combat the increasing difficulty of monster waves.

The game features a variety of environments, enemies, and power-ups to discover. With its simple controls but deep strategic elements, Monster Survivors is easy to learn but difficult to master - perfect for both casual gamers and hardcore enthusiasts alike.
